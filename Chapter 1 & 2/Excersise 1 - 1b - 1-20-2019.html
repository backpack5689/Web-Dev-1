Imagine Dragons

Who's in it?

There are 4 parts in the Imagine Dragons group. Currently there is:

  • Dan Reynolds: The lead singer from Las Vegas, NV
  • Wayne Sermon: Lead guitarist from American Fork, UT
  • Daniel Platzman: The drummer for the group, from Atlanta, GA
  • Ben McKee:The very important bass player from Forestville, CA

What have they done?

Imagine Dragons have done a lot of albums in the past, and the following are the 3 of my favorite albums.

Origins
Origins, being the newest on this list, definetly brings some great songs together under one album name. This
album has a lot of great songs with the same kind of vibe. It also includes zero from the new motion picture
"Ralph Breaks the Internet". All in all, you can't go wrong with this album!
Night Visions
This is the oldest album that they have on this list, releasing in 2012. However, if you don't have it in your
library already, that just makes it an even bigger issue! This album has great songs like the super popular song
"Radioactive", and some hidden gems like "Bleeding Out" and "Demons". By far my favorite
album, you should definetly go out and buy this album!
Smoke + Mirrors
This is my third favorite album, and for good reason. The music in this album just really forces someone to have
to think about their situation, while being super catchy. They all just hold so much meaning, and not just for the
authors as well. "Shots", "I Bet my Life", and "I'm so Sorry" have all helped me get through
things that has happend in my life. All in all, a great album filled to the brim with meaning!

The group has also gone on to create so many beautiful works of art, but these are just 3 of the many albums that I think should
be in everyones iTunes account. Speaking of...
